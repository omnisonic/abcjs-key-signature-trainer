About this Trainer

This trainer cycles either key signatures or individual notes on a staff so you can practice reading and identifying pitches.

Solfege (Movable Do): shows the scale degree name for each note relative to the key — learning to identify the "Do" on the staff improves sight-singing, relative pitch, and quick interval recognition.

Ledger lines: enable extended notes above and below the staff so you can practice reading notes across a wider range.
